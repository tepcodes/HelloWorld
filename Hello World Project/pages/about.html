How it started

In 2020, our family felt the effect of the pandemic hard. I refused to work in the hospital fearing that I may bring the virus to our house. To keep my parent safe, I tried out other jobs that allows for remote work. I quickly learned that in order to land a remote job, I need to have a skill. I tried to capitalize my skill in writing and in 2021 I was given an opportunity to be a content creator for a European company. I spent 2 years with that company until 2023.

While working as a content creator, I was working with the web developer of the company giving suggestions and discussing features that can be added to the website. I had a peak of what happens in the background and gained a lot of curiosity of how things are working.

How I am learning

Soon after, I tried learning on my own by reading articles, doing research on topics, and watching YouTube videos but to no avail. I cannot follow the information because I was not following a structured learning. I convinced my fried, who is a web developer himself, to mentor me. He gave me resources that I need to read and encouraged me to do my own research about terms I can't understand. I did that for a while but I still cannot digest all those information.

In July of 2023, my brother got married and I met his friend who was a web developer, too. I asked him to be my mentor and he agreed. He sent me the link to TheOdinProject. I am continuing my journey with TOP.

I am also using a pretty fun app, Mimo:Learn to Code. It is a fun app where I am introduced to bite-sized information that is very digestible.
